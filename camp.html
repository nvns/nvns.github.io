NVNS Summer Camp 2015 with Teacher Annie

Noe Valley Nursery School is offering 8 weeks of Summer Camp fun this year. Each week (max.)10 kids, who are 3-5 years old will spend wonderful days with Teacher Annie and an Assistant Teacher. Camp day will be from 9AM to 1PM. Parents are asked to pack lunch for their child, snack will be provided by the school.

Camp Director: Annie Bertram

In 2012, Annie obtained her Master's Degree in Early Childhood Education at George Fox University, located just outside of Portland, Oregon. She has taught at Noe Valley Nursery School for two years and served as Director for the school's fun and successful summer camp in 2013 & 2014. She is also in charge of the Afternoon program at NVNS. Annie comes from a large family of teachers and has grown up in various classroom environments. She loves going on hikes and preparing creative activities for the children. Annie is excited for what is to come this summer and can't wait to continue the long-standing traditions and community of Noe Valley Nursery School.

June Assistant Teacher: Dorothy Hart Grasle

Dorothy Hart Grasle used to be a preschool teacher and has a ton of experience with children, she is a babysitter for foster care children now and raised four kids of her own.

July Assistant Teacher: Eva Dunder

Eva Dunder is a junior at Tulane University studying Public Health with a focus in Child and Maternal health. She has done babysitting for many families.

Themes

Week 1 (June 1-5) Math Madness

We will discover new games and challenges by using special materials to take a closer look at the world of math. We will uncover numbers, patterns, geometry and measurement.

Week 2 (June 8-12) Words with Friends:

This unique opportunity will allow children to develop literacy through books, stories and acting. We will be using props such as puppets and costumes as well.

Week 3 (June 15-19) Exploring Art:

Together we will nurture creativity through art by exploring with color, line, forms, design, space and texture. We will also examine the works of famous artists that have influenced art as we know it.

Week 4 (June 22-26) Nature and the World Around Us:

Join us on an adventure while we take a closer look at nature and science in our very own backyard. Inspired by the Glen Canyon and the garden surrounding Noe Valley Nursery School.

Note that there is no summer school on the week leading to the 4th of July weekend, June 29th to July 3rd.

Week 5 (July 6-10) Math Madness:

We will retouch on our games and challenges in the world of math. We will be using toys, songs and puzzles to learn even more about counting, categorizing, and shapes.

Week 6 (July 13-17) Words with Friends:

The focus of this week is fun with comprehension. Together we will read, tell, and discuss stories. Children will get the chance to create new stories and add onto our plays from week 2.

Week 7 (July 20-24) Exploring Art:

Children will express themselves through art with drawing, painting, stamping, sculpturing, and collaging. As a group we will create individual pieces, as well as one giant masterpiece together. Throughout the week, we will incorporate our whole bodies with music and dance.

Week 8 (July 27-31) Nature and the World Around Us:

Learning through play and careful observation, campers will explore their connection to nature and to each other. The children will learn to foster a deeper appreciation and respect for nature and develop their sense of stewardship. You can expect your child to come home tired, dirty and with lots of adventures to tell you about!
