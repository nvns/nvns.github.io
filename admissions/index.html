How to Enroll

First, attend a Parent Information Night where parents and the Director will provide information about ourphilosophy, curriculum, and family responsibilities.

Next, schedule a visit to the school with your child, where you can observe your child in the school environment and have a one-on-one meeting with the Director.

Finally, send in the application form with a $40 non-refundable fee.

Our membership coordinator will contact you when a spot is available. If there is no current opening, your child will be placed into the waiting pool.

Our next parent information nights will take place on the following dates:

  • Thursday - November 12th 7:30 - 8:30pm
  • Tuesday - November 17th 7:30 - 8:30pm

If you are interested in enrolling for the upcoming 2021-2022 school year, or this year starting in January, please contact membership@noevalleynurseryschool.com.

Monthly Tuition

Tuition is due at the first parent meeting of each month and is based upon the number of days per week that the child attends.

Please contact our membership coordinator for additional information on tuition

Scholarships are available by request. Requests for tuition reduction must be submitted in writing to the Board of Directors.

Ages

Children must be between 2 years 9 months - 5 years old to attend Noe Valley Nursery School. Children must be potty trained upon enrollment. We do accept rolling enrollments, but the child must be age appropriate at the beginning of the school year.
