How to Enroll

First, attend a Parent Information Night where parents and the director will provide information about parent responsibilities, cost and the developmentally appropriate curriculum.

Then, if you would like, you can schedule a visit to the school with your child where you will have an opportunity to observe your child in the school environment. You can either fill in the application form with a $30 non-refundable fee at the Information night or take the form with you and mail it in.

Our membership coordinator will contact you when a spot is available. If there is no current opening, then your child will be place into the waiting pool.

Please email membership@noevalleynurseryschool.com to find out when the next parent information night will be held.

Tuition

Tuition is due at the first parent meeting of each month, and is based upon the number of days per week that the child attends:

  • For 5 days per week: $389
  • For 4 days per week: $314
  • For 3 days per week: $237

Scholarships are available by request. Requests for tuition reduction must be submitted in writing to the Board of Directors, and are given at their sole discretion.

Ages

Children must be 33 months - 5 years to attend Noe Valley Nursery School. Additionally, children must be potty trained upon enrollment. We do accept rolling enrollments but must be age appropriate at the beginning of the school year.
