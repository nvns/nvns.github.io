Cocktails for a Cause

The Cocktails for a Cause event is our primary fundraiser for the year. Funds raised will go to pay our teachers and rent, and will allow us to maintain a quality school, to keep tuition low, and to offer scholarships and financial aid, so San Francisco families of all financial backgrounds can get a quality, preschool education for their children. Please support us!

Cocktails for a Cause takes place the week before Thanksgiving. It's a great opportunity to wrap up all your holiday shopping at one, fell swoop with great deals on local items. We are a non-profit, so everything is a tax write-off, receipts provided. Click to view our Auction Catalog to see what will be up for bid.

Can't Attend? Donate through PayPal

You may make a cash donation to our fundraiser securely through your PayPal account. Thank you!

Mustaches for NVNS Fundraiser

In conjunction with our Cocktails for a Cause fundraiser, we are also holding a Mustaches for NVNS fundraiser. How does it work? On October 15th, the men of NVNS will start with a clean shaven face, and will compete with each other for the following five weeks for the sweetest 'stache. You can follow their week-by-week progress by 'liking' the NVNS Facebook page and viewing the Mustaches Photo Album. (You must have a Facebook account.) We will crown a Mustache King at our Cocktails for a Cause event, and the mustache which raises the most donations will also receive an award.

Please consider supporting your favorite 'stache by clicking the "Donate" button above. Once you complete the payment details, on the next page where you review the donation there is an optional box called 'Note to Seller.' Please put "[Insert Your Favorite NVNS Man]'s Mustache" in that note so we can keep track of all the hairy fundraising. Thanks for your support!
