Cocktails for a Cause Fundraiser

Noe Valley Nursery School's 6th Annual Fundraiser, Cocktails for a Cause, will be held the evening of Saturday, November 21st, 2015 at the Verdi Club. The event will feature:

  • Silent and Live Auctions
  • Delicious Hors D'Ouevres
  • Live Music and a Hosted Bar
  • "Instant Cellar" Wine Raffle

Registration opening soon - stay tuned!

Mustaches for NVNS Fundraiser

In conjunction with our Cocktails for a Cause fundraiser, we are also holding a Mustaches for NVNS fundraiser. How does it work? On October 15th, the men of NVNS will start with a clean shaven face, and will compete with each other for the following five weeks for the sweetest 'stache. You can follow their week-by-week progress by 'liking' the NVNS Facebook page and viewing the Mustaches Photo Album. (You must have a Facebook account.) We will crown a Mustache King at our Cocktails for a Cause event, and the mustache which raises the most donations will also receive an award.

Please consider supporting your favorite 'stache by clicking the "Donate" button below. Once you complete the payment details, on the next page where you review the donation there is an optional box called 'Note to Seller.' Please put "[Insert Your Favorite NVNS Man]'s Mustache" in that note so we can keep track of all the hairy fundraising. Thanks for your support!
